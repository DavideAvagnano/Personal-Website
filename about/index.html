A little about me

Hi, I'm Davide 👋

01.

Educational Background

At the age of 23, my unconventional journey led me from aerospace engineering to programming and code writing. After graduating, I chose to discontinue my studies during the master's degree. For two years, I worked as a lifeguard in various settings. This experience taught me the importance of dedication and responsibility, exposing me to significant challenges that shaped my resilient mindset. However, I knew from the start that it was a temporary job, as my interest had shifted to the world of programming.

02.

Transition to Coding and Problem-Solving Aptitude

With a strong aptitude for problem-solving since university days, coding became the tangible expression of my passion. Over time, I've explored various areas, from data analysis, given my university focus on statistics and probability, to web development. I've had the opportunity to get my hands dirty with different programming languages and various related software. I would love to actively engage in the realization of interesting projects that can stimulate me and satisfy my thirst for knowledge.

03.

Balanced Lifestyle and Travel Enthusiast

Advocating for a balanced lifestyle, I incorporate yoga and meditation into my daily routine. Outdoors sports are integral to my life, as the connection with nature is fundamental. Shaped by the influence of a flight attendant parent, travel has always been my way of discovering the world. The allure of the unknown intrigues me, making exploration a central passion.

04.

Passion for Finance and Blockchain Technology

Actively investing in various sectors, from stocks to cryptocurrencies, finance fascinates me. My fascination extends to blockchain technology, leading me to participate in conferences and a Master's program in Rome.
